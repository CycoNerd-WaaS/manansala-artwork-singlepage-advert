The Artwork

A Rhythmic Feast of the Senses

In this complex and intimate composition, Manansala transforms a traditional Filipino dinner setting into a celestial dance of form and light. The canvas captures a lush bounty of succulent shrimp, seafood, and seasonal fruits, rendered with the rhythmic grace that defined his career.

Utilizing his mastery of Transparent Cubism, the artist layers translucent planes of color to reveal the "inner soul" of the objects. Each element - from the delicate curve of the shrimp to the pulp of the fruit and the surrounding vessels - is refracted, allowing the viewer to see through and beyond the physical form into a harmonious, luminous whole. This work turns a domestic scene into a profound exploration of light, texture, and cultural identity.
